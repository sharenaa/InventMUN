Committees

INVENTMUN offers a variety of committees designed to challenge delegates to think critically, collaborate effectively, and engage in meaningful debate.

General Assembly

Focuses on global issues such as international security, human rights, and sustainable development.

Specialized Committees

These committees explore specific topics and require deeper research and creative problem-solving.

Crisis Committees

Fast-paced and interactive committees where delegates must respond quickly to evolving situations.

Committee assignments will be released prior to the conference.
