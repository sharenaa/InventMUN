InventMUN COMMITTEE POLICIES

Attendance Policy

    Attendance will be taken at the beginning of each Committee Session, if a delegate is absent from their committee, their advisor will be notified.
Delegates may not visit other committees or leave their own during unmoderated caucuses.

Notes Policy

    Delegates are encouraged to bring notes to committee, such as their topic guide, to aid them in debate.
    Pre-written materials, such as draft resolutions, working papers or crisis notes, are not allowed. Delegates may not bring them in as “notes’’. Any delegate found with pre-written documents will be disqualified for awards and their advisor will be informed.

Technology Policy

    Phones are NOT ALLOWED in committee. If a delegate is caught using their phone, they will be disqualified from awards.
    Delegates are only to use their laptops, Ipads, and other devices(excluding phones) during designated Unmoderated Caucuses. Delegates MAY NOT use these devices during Moderated Caucuses for any reason, including accessing their notes, presenting speeches, taking notes, etc.
    Delegates are only allowed to work on their working papers and resolution papers during committee. If a delegate is found to have worked on their working papers and resolution papers outside of committee, the whole paper will be disqualified and that delegate will be disqualified from awards.

AI Policy

    All and any use of AI is prohibited at InventMUN.
    Delegates may not use AI in the preparation and production of materials for committee including but not limited to notes, speech writing, crisis arcs, crisis notes, and questions for other delegates.
    If any prewritten or printed material is found to have been created using AI, these materials will be taken and the delegate may be ineligible for awards.
    Delegates may not use AI during committees, if a delegate is caught using AI in any form, they will be disqualified from awards. If the use of AI is detected on working papers or resolution papers, those papers will be disqualified and the delegate of that bloc will be ineligible for awards.

Behavior Policy

    Delegates must engage in debate in a professional and respectful manner.
    Bullying, Discrimination, and Harassment are strictly prohibited at InventMUN. Mistreatment of fellow delegates, chairs, or Secretariat members will result in disqualifications of awards AND removal from committee( with the potential to be removed from the conference at the Secretariat’s discretion).
    Vulgar language is NOT tolerated.
    Repeated infractions will result in removal from the conference.
