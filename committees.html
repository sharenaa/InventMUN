Committee Policies

Attendance Policy

Attendance will be taken at the beginning of each committee session. Delegates who are absent will have their advisor notified. Delegates may not leave their assigned committee or visit other committees during unmoderated caucuses.

Notes Policy

Delegates are encouraged to bring handwritten or printed notes, including their topic guide, to assist them during debate. Pre-written materials such as draft resolutions, working papers, or crisis notes are not permitted.

Any delegate found in possession of pre-written materials will be disqualified from awards and their advisor will be informed.

Technology Policy

Phones are not permitted in committee at any time. Delegates found using phones will be disqualified from awards.

Laptops and tablets may only be used during designated unmoderated caucuses for working paper and resolution writing. Devices may not be used during moderated caucuses for any purpose.

AI & Academic Integrity Policy

The use of artificial intelligence, plagiarism, or pre-written materials is strictly prohibited at InventMUN.

Behavior Policy

Delegates are expected to engage in debate in a professional and respectful manner at all times. Bullying, discrimination, harassment, or vulgar language will not be tolerated.
