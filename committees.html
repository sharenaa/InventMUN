InventMUN Committee Policies

To ensure a fair, respectful, and productive conference experience, all delegates are expected to follow the policies outlined below.

Attendance Policy

  • Attendance will be taken at the beginning of each committee session.
  • If a delegate is absent, their advisor will be notified.
  • Delegates may not visit other committees or leave their own during unmoderated caucuses.

Notes Policy

  • Delegates are encouraged to bring topic guides and handwritten notes.
  • Pre-written materials such as draft resolutions, working papers, or crisis notes are not allowed.
  • Any delegate found with pre-written documents will be disqualified from awards and their advisor will be informed.

Technology Policy

  • Phones are not permitted in committee.
  • Delegates caught using phones will be disqualified from awards.
  • Laptops and tablets may only be used during unmoderated caucuses.
  • Technology may not be used during moderated caucuses for any reason.
  • Delegates may only work on papers during committee sessions.
  • Any work completed outside committee will result in disqualification of the paper and delegate.

AI Policy

  • The use of AI is strictly prohibited at InventMUN.
  • AI may not be used to prepare notes, speeches, resolutions, crisis arcs, or questions.
  • Materials found to be created using AI will be confiscated.
  • Delegates caught using AI will be disqualified from awards.
  • Resolution papers created using AI will be disqualified.

Behavior Policy

  • Delegates must conduct themselves professionally and respectfully at all times.
  • Bullying, discrimination, and harassment are strictly prohibited.
  • Vulgar or inappropriate language will not be tolerated.
  • Severe or repeated violations may result in removal from committee or the conference.
