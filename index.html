InventMUN Innovation · Diplomacy · Leadership

Thomas A. Edison CTE High School is proud to introduce InventMUN, its inaugural Model United Nations learning conference, taking place on March 28, 2026, at 165-65 84th Avenue in Jamaica, New York. Created as a free and accessible conference for delegates of all experience levels, InventMUN aims to provide students with a meaningful introduction to diplomacy, debate, and international affairs.

Guided by Edison’s core value “There is no substitute for hard work,” the conference encourages delegates to think critically, communicate effectively, and collaborate with peers as they work to address complex global challenges.

InventMUN is delighted to collaborate with CESIMS as they pilot their Conference Operations Fellowship (COF), an initiative aimed at strengthening newly developed Model United Nations conferences among secondary institutions. Through this partnership, CESIMS will provide structured mentorship to the secretariat as they organize the conference, as well as training sessions for the chairs and staff. This partnership aims to offer guidance and mentorship to the InventMUN secretariat to ensure a successful and enriching conference for all.

This is where diplomacy gets built.
