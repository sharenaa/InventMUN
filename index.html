InventMUN Innovation · Diplomacy · Leadership

InventMUN is a student-run Model United Nations conference where policy meets possibility. Hosted by Thomas A. Edison Career & Technical Education High School, InventMUN challenges delegates to approach global issues with creativity, technical thinking, and real-world perspective.

Inspired by Edison’s legacy of invention and problem-solving, our conference pushes delegates to do more than debate solutions — it asks them to design them. From emerging technologies to sustainability and international security, InventMUN is a space for bold ideas, thoughtful diplomacy, and the next generation of global leaders.

This is where diplomacy gets built.
