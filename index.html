InventMUN Innovation · Diplomacy · Leadership

InventMUN is a student-run Model United Nations conference where policy meets possibility. Hosted by Thomas A. Edison Career & Technical Education High School, InventMUN challenges delegates to approach global issues with creativity, technical thinking, and real-world perspective.

Inspired by Edison’s legacy of invention and problem-solving, our conference pushes delegates to do more than debate solutions — it asks them to design them. InventMUN is Thomas A. Edison CTE High School’s first learning conference, grounded in Edison’s core value: “There’s no substitute for hard work.” This conference is designed to be a space where delegates can learn, ask questions, challenge ideas, and grow as thinkers and student leaders. From emerging technologies to sustainability and international security, InventMUN holds bold ideas, thoughtful diplomacy, and the next generation of global leaders.

This is where diplomacy gets built.
