CESIMS

Press Release

InventMUN is delighted to collaborate with CESIMS as they pilot their Conference Operations Fellowship (COF), an initiative aimed at strengthening newly developed Model United Nations conferences among secondary institutions.

Through this partnership, CESIMS will provide structured mentorship to the secretariat as they organize the conference, as well as training sessions for the chairs and staff. This partnership aims to offer guidance and mentorship to the InventMUN secretariat to ensure a successful and enriching conference for all.
